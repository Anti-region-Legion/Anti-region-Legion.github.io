The Anti-#region Legion

The Anti-#region Legion is a community for people that believe that the C# #region is a unfortunate language construct.

If you just love your regions The Anti-#region Legion is probably not for you. But it is suggested that you read Richard Banks post on some of the issues with regions.

How do I join the Legion?

You join by starring this repository.

History

The idea for the anti-#region legion started from a twitter conversation which again was started from Microsoft declining to remove #regions from c#.

Join the anti-#region Legion today!
